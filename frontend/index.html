Fill The Gaps

Welcome!

In this Wild Card game you are presented with a sequence of letters separated by '*' characters.

Each '*' character means there is a gap of zero or more letters you need to identify.

Your job is to find a word that matches the pattern!

Just like a cryptography expert that knows how to crack codes, or a Machine Learning Algorithm that can detect patterns..

We test your problem solving skills, and your vocabulary knowledge! You have 2 minutes to fill as many gaps as possible!

Good luck!
